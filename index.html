Pack-by-LED

Streamlining shipping operations

Pack-by-LED is a fast, visual way to guide warehouse operators during the packing process. Instead of buttons or complex screens, LEDs show what to pack next: simple, efficient, and plug & play.

From Pick-by-Light to Pack-by-LED

Traditional pick-by-light systems rely on physical buttons at every location. We initially explored that approach but quickly found it not a suitable fit. During the packing process, operators already use a PDA or handheld scanner, so adding buttons only makes things slower and more expensive.

Pack-by-LED eliminates the button completely. The LEDs are controlled directly by the software, triggered by scan events or shipment status. This makes the system faster, cheaper, easier to install, and much more flexible than traditional pick-by-light systems.

Key Benefits

  • Accuracy: One glance. Pack. Next.
  • Speed: Instant LED feedback keeps operators moving without hesitation.
  • Ease of use: Intuitive and easy to learn. No training needed.
  • Plug & Play: Quick to install, no special hardware required.

How it works

  • Uses addressable LED strips connected to small Wi-Fi controllers.
  • Controlled by a lightweight software service running on a local device or server.
  • Connects to databases, APIs or CSV exports from ERP or WMS systems.
  • LEDs update in real time based on shipment progress.
  • Hardware is low-cost, modular and easy to expand.
