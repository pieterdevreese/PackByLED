Pack-by-LED

Streamlining Warehouse Operations

What it is

Pack-by-LED is a simple, visual system that guides warehouse operators during packing using LEDs. Each LED indicates exactly which item to pack and when — reducing errors and speeding up the process.

Key Benefits

  • Faster Packing: Operators immediately see what to pack—no need to check screens or paperwork.
  • Reduced Errors: Clear LED guidance minimizes mistakes.
  • Intuitive & Easy: Works like a “traffic light” system for packing—minimal training required.
  • Flexible & Scalable: Works for single or multi-item shipments and can adapt to any warehouse layout.
  • Data Insights: Tracks performance, station utilization, and bottlenecks.

How it works (High-Level Tech)

  • Hardware: Affordable addressable LED strips connected to small Wi-Fi controllers.
  • Network: Runs on standard Wi-Fi—no complex infrastructure required.
  • Software: Lightweight controller app runs on a local server or standalone device.
  • Integration: Connects to existing systems via database, API, or simple CSV files.
  • Real-Time Control: LEDs update instantly based on shipment status—showing when an order is ready, packed, or shipped.
  • Plug & Play: Quick setup, minimal wiring, and ready to use within minutes.

Why it matters

Traditional packing relies on screens, scanners, or paperwork. Pack-by-LED replaces all that with one glance—faster, leaner, and more intuitive.
