Pack-by-LED

Streamlining Warehouse Operations

Pack-by-LED is a fast, visual way to guide warehouse operators during the packing process. Instead of buttons or complex screens, LEDs show what to pack next — simple, efficient, and plug & play.

The Challenge

In many warehouses, picking is done across multiple zones. Before shipping, all items must be consolidated into one shipment. At the same time, there are different cut-off and shipping times depending on the customer, destination and carrier.

Operators currently have to check small PDA or handheld scanner screens to see which shipments are complete and ready for packing. This means searching through racks and lists before actually starting to pack and ship — a time-consuming and error-prone process.

The Solution

With Pack-by-LED, operators no longer have to check screens. When all items for a shipment are present in the rack, the LEDs light up automatically.

The color of the LED indicates the cut-off time or shipping priority:

Green – immediate shipment
Blue – standard shipment
Orange – later cut-off

This allows operators to instantly see which shipments are ready and what to prioritize — no searching, no scanning, no delays.

From Pick-by-Light to Pack-by-LED

Traditional pick-by-light systems rely on physical buttons at every location. We initially explored that approach but found it unnecessary. During the packing process, operators already use a PDA or handheld scanner, so adding buttons only makes things slower and more expensive.

Pack-by-LED eliminates the button completely. The LEDs are controlled directly by the packing software, triggered by scan events or shipment status. This makes the system faster, cheaper, easier to install, and much more flexible than traditional hardware.

Key Benefits

  • Speed: Immediate LED feedback keeps operators moving efficiently.
  • Accuracy: Each LED represents an action. Less confusion, fewer returns.
  • Ease of use: Intuitive and easy to learn. No extra hardware or training required.
  • Scalability: Works for one packing table or an entire warehouse.
  • Plug & Play: Quick to install, no special infrastructure needed.

How it works

  • Uses addressable LED strips connected to small Wi-Fi controllers.
  • Controlled by a lightweight software service running on a local device or server.
  • Connects to databases, APIs or CSV exports from ERP or WMS systems.
  • LEDs update in real time based on shipment progress.
  • Hardware is low-cost, modular and easy to expand.

Why companies like it

Pack-by-LED bridges the gap between digital systems and physical work. It brings visual clarity and speed to packing operations while keeping hardware simple and affordable. Many warehouses start small and quickly expand once they see the impact.
